Publications

  • D. Zhu, D. He, Y. Zhou and Z. Liu, "Semantic Line Map: A Novel Visual Map Representation Scheme for Autonomous Driving Systems," 2023 6th International Conference on Intelligent Autonomous Systems (ICoIAS), Qinhuangdao, China, 2023, pp. 15-20.

    Proposed a novel high-precision map representation scheme called "Semantic Line Map (SEML)," which combines deep learning and line segment storage methods to improve storage efficiency and positioning speed in autonomous driving systems. The paper uses COLMAP combined with PSPNet to generate 3D semantic line maps from 2D images, demonstrating advantages in storage and localization efficiency.

  • D. Zhu, M. Zhang, S. Yang, D. He. "Collaborative Bearing and Navigation of Multiple Mobile Robots Based on ROS," Control Engineering, 2022, 29(03): 419-427.

    Addressed the challenge of collaborative handling of multiple mobile robots in narrow spaces by proposing a collaborative bearing and navigation scheme based on ROS, achieving coordinated cooperation and precise navigation of multiple robots. The distance-based formation control method ensures load balance posture, and global mapping and optimal path planning achieve efficient and stable multi-robot collaboration.
